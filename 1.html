What do I need to start bullet journaling?

A notebook

First, you can open your fine note! If it's comfortable for you to use, any notebook is okay.
If you don't have your comfy notebook, or want a fresh start, here is my reccomendation.

A pen

Next, you'll need a nice pen(s). And which pen should you use? It's up to you! You can pick your favorite-or any kind of pen-to write with.

Remember, this is your bulletjournal, so all choice is yours to make!
